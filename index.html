What is Guest Posting?

Guest posting (also called guest blogging) is a content marketing and SEO strategy where you write and publish an article on another website. In return, you receive a dofollow backlink to your site, referral traffic, and increased brand authority.

It remains one of the most effective white-hat link-building strategies in 2026 — when done with high-quality, relevant content on authoritative sites.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is guest posting and why is it important for SEO? +
    Guest posting is writing an article for another website's blog in exchange for a backlink. It's one of the most powerful white-hat SEO strategies because backlinks from high-DA websites are a top Google ranking factor. A single guest post on a DA 70+ site can significantly move the needle on your search rankings.
  • Are there Paid guest posting sites available? +
    Yes! Many sites accept Paid guest posts, especially if your content is original, well-researched, and genuinely valuable to their readers. Our directory clearly labels each site as Paid or Paid so you can filter accordingly. Paid sites typically require higher content quality in lieu of payment.
  • What is Moz Domain Authority (DA)? +
    Moz Domain Authority (DA) is a search engine ranking score from 1 to 100 developed by Moz. It predicts how likely a website is to rank on search engine result pages. Backlinks from sites with higher DA scores pass more SEO value. We recommend targeting sites with DA 40+ for the best results.
  • How do I submit a guest post to these sites? +
    Click the "Visit Site" button next to any listing to go directly to that website. Look for their "Write For Us," "Guest Post," or "Contribute" page — usually linked in the footer or navigation. Read their submission guidelines carefully, pitch a unique and relevant topic via email or their contact form, wait for approval, then submit your full article.
  • How often is this directory updated? +
    We review and update this directory regularly — removing dead sites, adding new ones, and refreshing DA scores. The last update was June 2026. If you find a site that's no longer accepting guest posts, please contact us so we can remove it.
  • Is guest posting safe for SEO in 2026? +
    Yes — when done correctly. Google's guidelines allow guest posting as long as the content is genuinely helpful to readers, relevant to the host site's niche, and not keyword-stuffed. Avoid spammy link schemes, ensure articles are high quality (1000+ words recommended), and vary your anchor text naturally. The sites in our directory are editorially reviewed for quality.
